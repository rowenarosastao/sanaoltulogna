Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (released in the United States, India and the Philippines as Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone) is a 2001 fantasy film directed by Chris Columbus and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures, based on J. K. Rowling's 1997 novel of the same name.

Directed by:Chris Columbus

Screenplay by:Steve Kloves

Based on: Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone by J. K. Rowling

Produced by: David Heyman

Running time: 152 minutes

Language: English

Release Date (Streaming): November 4,2001
